District:Gaya

I am from district Gaya.Gaya is a city of ancient, historical and mythological significance. It is one of the major tourist attractions of the state of Bihar. Gaya is the 2nd largest city of Bihar,Situated on the banks of the Phalgu River (or Niranjana, as mentioned in Ramayana), it is a place sanctified by the Jain, Hindu, and Buddhist religions.

A soil of enlightenment, Gaya is a significant pilgrimage place that is steeped in great history.It is a ritual site and renowned for Pind Daan. Hindu devotees from all across the world pay a visit to this consecrated place to carry out Pind Daan pooja for the liberation of the souls of their relatives. Gotten its name from the demon Gayasur, it is certainly a striking city enveloped by temple-crowned mountains.
